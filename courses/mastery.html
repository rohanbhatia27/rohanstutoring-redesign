Which sections can the 1:1 tutorials cover?

All of them. You choose what to work on each session: S1 reasoning, S2 essay structure, exam-day strategy, study planning, or a specific question type you can't crack. The sessions are yours to use as you need.

How does unlimited essay marking work?

Submit as many essays as you like throughout the program. Each one comes back with line-level feedback within 48 hours. There is no cap and no waiting in a queue behind other students.

When is the study roadmap built?

Before the program starts. We'll ask about your background, your current scores, how much time you have each week, and when you're sitting. The roadmap is built around your situation and revisited at each monthly check-in as things change.

What's included from the Comprehensive Course?

Everything. All 24 live classes (S1 and S2), the same 50+ hour Blueprint library delivered via Google Drive, 250+ targeted S1 questions, live essay feedback sessions, the Expert Essay Collection, and direct access to Rohan throughout.

How does the refund guarantee work?

Attend at least 80% of classes and engage with the program. If you finish and don't feel more confident in your preparation, contact us for a full refund. No complicated process.

Can I join from the UK or Ireland?

Yes. We have students across Australia, the UK and Ireland. All live classes and private tutorials run online via Zoom. The AEDT schedule means UK and Irish students typically join in the morning.
