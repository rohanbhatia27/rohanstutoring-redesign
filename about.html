I know what it feels like to sit the GAMSAT and walk out frustrated.

When I started preparing, I had no idea where to go. I tried the big prep companies. I paid the prices. I kept getting stuck with feedback that told me nothing useful.

So I rebuilt my approach. I studied philosophy, psychology, and literature to understand what the GAMSAT actually tests. I built a framework that turned vague comments like "lacks depth" into steps I could act on under exam conditions.

Eventually, I scored in the top 1% of GAMSAT students and got into the University of Sydney. I'm now in my third year of medicine.

Since then, I've coached 900+ students across Australia, the UK and Ireland. I started Rohan's Tutoring because the existing options were charging $5,000 for recycled content. I wanted to build something useful at a price that didn't require a loan.

Why We're Different

The things we won't compromise on.

01

Current knowledge, not recycled content

Our tutors sat the exam recently. Every framework we teach is based on the current version of the test. We don't repackage old materials and call them premium.

02

Affordable without cutting corners

The big companies charge $3,000–$5,000+. We charge a fraction of that. Not because we cut corners, but because we refuse to build a business on students who are already under financial pressure.
