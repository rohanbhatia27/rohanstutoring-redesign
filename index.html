What students ask before enrolling

Do I need a science background?

No. Our methods work for students from any background including arts, commerce, and science. The GAMSAT rewards reasoning and communication, not memorised content. We teach you how to think, not what to recall.

How is this different from the big prep companies?

Big prep companies charge $3,000 to $5,000+ for prerecorded lectures, recycled question banks, and tutors who sat the GAMSAT a decade ago. You get dropped into a 60-person Zoom, treated like a row on a spreadsheet, and left chasing replies from someone who never learned your name.

We work differently. I'm Rohan, a current USyd med student, and I lead every cohort myself. Our tutors are top 1% scorers and current med students who sat this exam recently, so they know what's actually being tested now, not the version from 2014. The team stays small on purpose. Lower overhead is why our courses start at a fraction of theirs. Smaller cohorts are why your essays get read, your weak sections get worked on, and your messages get answered the same day.

You don't need a $5,000 course to get into medical school. You need someone who knows the current exam, knows you, and is genuinely invested in your result.

Can I join if I'm based in the UK or Ireland?

Yes. We have a significant cohort of UK and Irish students. All sessions are online and scheduled around time zones. We know the GAMSAT application requirements for Irish and UK medical schools.

What if I've already sat the GAMSAT multiple times?

The Advanced Mastery Series is built for resitters. If you've hit a score ceiling, the problem is usually strategic, not effort. We'll work out what's holding you back and build a plan around that, not a generic curriculum.
